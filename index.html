Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj

5th Original Jagadguru of the World | The Reconciler of All Scriptures

He appeared to drown the world in the ocean of Divine Love. His philosophy reconciles all contradictory views of world scriptures and reveals the simplest path to God realization: Raganuga Bhakti (Selfless Love).

1922

The Divine Appearance

Appeared on Sharad Purnima night in Mangarh, Pratapgarh. From childhood, his divine radiance captivated everyone around him.

1957

Honored as Jagadgurottam

At just 34 years old, he stunned the scholars of Kashi Vidvat Parishat. They unanimously honored him as the 5th Original Jagadguru, a title given only 4 times before in history.

Scriptures

The Ocean of Knowledge

He gifted the world with monumental works like Prem Ras Madira (1008 pads), Radha Govind Geet, and Prem Ras Siddhant, simplifying the Vedas for the common man.

Legacy

Temples of Love

He established three divine monuments: Prem Mandir (Vrindavan), Bhakti Mandir (Mangarh), and Kirti Mandir (Barsana) to preserve devotion for eternity.

The Eternal Inspiration

It is his divine vision that inspired Devi Ji to establish Radha Govind Dham in the Himalayas, carrying forward the flag of Bhakti.

Sushri Braj Bhuvaneshwari Devi Ji

The Reflection of Grace | Dedicated Pracharika

"In her conduct, speech, and every action, one sees only Shri Maharaj Ji."
She is the embodiment of surrender. Born to a family of devotees, she left worldly academia to dedicate her entire life to distributing the nectar of Bhakti Yoga as per her Master's command.

1971

Divine Appearance

Appeared on September 22 in Lucknow, UP. Born into a divine family where her parents and siblings were already ardent devotees of Shri Maharaj Ji.

Early Life

Education & Renunciation

She excelled in academics, completing her M.A. and M.Phil. However, while pursuing her Ph.D., the color of Sahaj Vairagya (Natural Renunciation) took over, and she left it midway to serve her Guru.

1998

The Divine Command

On the auspicious day of Diwali, Shri Maharaj Ji formally declared her as his Pracharika and bestowed upon her the divine name "Braj Bhuvaneshwari".

Mission

Kripalu Padma Trust

Following Maharaj Ji's order, she established her first divine center, Shri Kripalu Kunj in Bathinda (Punjab), creating a foundation for Satsang in the region.

The Himalayan Vision

Expanding her mission, she established Radha Govind Dham in Chail, a sanctuary in the Himalayas for deep Sadhana and solitude.
