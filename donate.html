President

Sushri Braj Bhuvneshwari Devi Ji

(Affectionately known as Didi Ji)

One of the foremost preachers of Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj, she continues her selfless service by building Radha Govind Dham at Chail and nurturing a spiritual Ashram in Bathinda, Punjab, for seekers on the path of devotion.

Our Mission

  • Spiritual Upliftment: To promote spiritual growth through satsang, meditation, sādhanā shivirs, and the study of sacred scriptures, fostering moral and ethical living for all.
  • Philanthropy & Seva: To serve society through charitable activities including education, healthcare, food distribution, financial assistance, and support for the needy with a spirit of selfless seva.
  • Preservation: To preserve and promote spiritual heritage, scriptures, teachings, and sacred spaces for the benefit of present and future generations.

Your Seva in Action

We believe in complete transparency. Here is how your contributions are transforming lives and building the Dham.

Building the Sanctuary

Every donation helps procure stone, marble, and labor for the main Temple structure. We are currently finalizing the inner sanctum (Garbhagriha) where the deities will reside.

Annadanam (Prasad)

No one leaves the Ashram hungry. Funds ensure that daily Bhog is offered to the Lord and distributed as Prasad to all devotees, visitors, and the local needy population.

Winter Warmth Drive

Distributed 1000+ clothes to the needy people of Bathinda ,Punjab.
